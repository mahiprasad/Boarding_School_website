About

Ecole Globale is one of the best alternatives to consider. They emphasize a great deal one thing before they start academics: INDEPENDENCE. Girls are now more independent and self-sufficient. It is a well-known decision for guardians who look for confirmation regarding their girl(s) security, education, and professional foundation. This school focuses on the general improvement of the girl student and raises the bar for women’s education in the country. It is amongst the best international schools in India for young ladies with splendid scholastic projects and magnificent grounds of educational and sports framework. This foundation is focused on strengthening young women in the nation, and this drives them to be the best every year. The school site has all the data you may need to comprehend with respect to why this school merits as the best Girls Boarding School in India. Ecole Globale was established by Mr. Amarjeet Juneja., under the umbrella of the Asian Educational Charitable Trust, in April 2012, and is associated with the Cambridge International Examinations (CIE) and the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). With this certainty in mind, this school is sincerely dedicated to empowering the girls of the country. With their excellent educational programs and unconventional teaching methods, they are a popular choice for every parent who wishes to send their girls to a boarding school.
